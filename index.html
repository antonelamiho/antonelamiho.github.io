Working and policy papers

Miho, Antonela
Small screen, big echo? Estimating the political persuasion of local television news bias using the Sinclair Broadcast Group as a natural experiment
Working paper, last version November 2022.
This paper investigates the heterogeneous effect of biased local TV news on political outcomes and opinions in the United States, exploiting the timing of the introduction of biased programming of a conservative-leaning broadcasting company, Sinclair Broadcast Group, over the years 1992-2020.
